Bookologia is not responsible for broken links, unavailable content, or external website policies. We do not endorse or guarantee the accuracy of the information found on linked sites.
4. Copyright & DMCA
Bookologia is a specialized search engine that indexes and organizes links to book-related content retrieved from publicly available sources, specifically Google and DuckDuckGo. We do not host, sell, distribute, or provide direct access to any books or copyrighted material. Our database consists solely of links gathered from these search engines. Since Bookologia does not host or control the content linked on external sites, we can only remove links from our index upon valid copyright requests.
If you believe that a copyrighted work is being unlawfully distributed, we recommend taking the following steps:

  1. Contact the website that is hosting the infringing material directly and request its removal.
  2. Submit a copyright complaint to the search engines that index and provide access to the content:
    • Google DMCA Report
    • DuckDuckGo DMCA Report
  3. Contact Bookologia to request the removal of the link from our search results.

To submit a DMCA takedown request to Bookologia, send an email to "bookologiaproject @ hotmail.com" with the subject line: "Book Link DMCA Takedown". Your email must include the following details:
  • An identification of the copyrighted work(s) or trademark(s) that you claim have been infringed.
  • A description of the nature and location of the work(s) that you claim to be infringing, including the URL(s) where the work(s) are located.
  • A statement affirming that you have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the rights holder, its agent, or the law.
  • A declaration that the information provided in your DMCA Notice is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are the rights holder or are authorized to act on their behalf.

Please note that even if we remove a link from our search index, it may reappear if the original source remains accessible via general search engines such as Google or DuckDuckGo. Our search engine database is refreshed every 15 days, meaning that if infringing content remains publicly indexed by other search engines, it may be re-added to our search results. To ensure complete removal, steps (1) and (2) should be taken before contacting us. For further transparency, our search engine's indexing process is open-source. You may review the details of how our system works on our GitHub repository: GitHub - Bookologia .
5. Privacy

Session cookies are destroyed immediately after log out action. No additional user data is stored. Users may request account deletion at any time. We do not share, or sell or use the user's data in any form other than providing services offereed by Bookologia.
